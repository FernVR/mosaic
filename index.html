A few words from our Founder

I am a South African/ Irish artist and creator living in Kilkenny, Ireland. I grew up in a rural area, where it seemed as if there was no room for diversity. Especially in creative scenes. The people I looked to for creative guidance were people who didn't look like me or share any of my experiences. This is a lonely, isolating experience for young people like me, and I've always wanted to try to spark change. As I've grown up, lived and worked in many different parts of the world, I've connected to my heritage and the importance of creating inclusivity in spaces around me. I created this site with the goal of connecting people through art, media and challenging the structures we grew up with. Mosaic is a safe space, where all people can discover and share new art, as well appreciate and connect to a diverse point of view. All are welcome to our friendly space and we hope that you enjoy browsing and hopefully connecting to our wonderful coterie.

Fern van Rooyen, Founder
