Our Mission

I am a South African/Irish artist and creator living in Kilkenny. I grew up in a rurual area, where it seemed there wasn't enough room for diversity. In creative scenes, the people I looked to for guidance and advice where people who didn't look like me, or share any of my experiences. This can be a lonely and isolating experience for young people and I've always wanted to try to change this.

I've grown up and lived around the world and I've connected deepy to my heritage and the importance of creating inclusivity in spaces around me. I've created this site with a goal of connecting people through art, media and challenging structures we've grown up with. Mosaic is a safe space for all, where people can discover and share new art and connect to a diverse point of view. We hope you enjoy browsing and connecting to our coeterie.

Fern van Rooyen, Founder
