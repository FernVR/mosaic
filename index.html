A few words from our Founder

I am a South African/ Irish artist and creator living in Kilkenny, Ireland. In rural Kilkenny in the early 2000s, there wasn't a lot of room for diversity. Especially in creative scenes. The people I looked to for creative guidance were usually white and male. I knew this had to change. As I've grown up, lived and worked in many different parts of the world, I feel more connected to my mixed-race roots and the importance of creating inclusivity in spaces around me. I created this site with the goal of connecting people through art, media and challenging the structures we grew up with. Mosaic is a safe space, where all people can discover and share new art, as well appreciate and connect to a diverse point of view. All are welcome to our friendly space and we hope that you enjoy browsing and hopefully connecting to our wonderful coterie.


Fern van Rooyen, Founder
